  <section id="intro">
    <h2>Welcome!</h2>
    <p>Feeling tired, stressed, or unmotivated? You might be experiencing academic burnout. You're not alone — and there are ways to manage it.</p>
  </section>

  <section id="symptoms">
    <h2>Symptoms of Burnout</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Constant fatigue</li>
      <li>Lack of motivation</li>
      <li>Decreased academic performance</li>
      <li>Feeling detached or hopeless</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section id="causes">
    <h2>Causes of Burnout</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Over-scheduling and lack of breaks</li>
      <li>Excessive screen time</li>
      <li>Pressure to succeed</li>
      <li>Neglecting sleep or nutrition</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section id="tips">
    <h2>How to Avoid Burnout</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Set realistic goals</li>
      <li>Take regular breaks (use the 50/10 rule)</li>
      <li>Sleep 7–8 hours daily</li>
      <li>Talk to someone – don’t bottle it up</li>
      <li>Practice mindfulness or light exercise</li>
    </ol>
  </section>
